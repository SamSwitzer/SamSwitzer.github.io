About the Artist

Art is something I have always appreciated. Growing up in the quieter Cornwall, Ontario, my parents surrounded me with different mediums and interests. At 24 now, I’ve found my passion in UI/UX design.

My name is Sam Switzer. I am an artist and developer. My art reminds others of what you would see in a concept artbook. When I’m not sketching flash sheets or using Adobe Suite, I am developing bot scripts or games for my friends to play. My inspirations include Yoji Shinkawa, Rockstar, Blizzard, and Source. My process is simply to find the passion behind what I’m working with, really focus in on my work, and continue to refine it for the rest of it’s life.
